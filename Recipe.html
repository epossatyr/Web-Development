Aubergine Rolls

Prepare a vegetarian dish everyone will enjoy

Ingredients
  • 2 Eggplants/Aubergine
  • 480g Fresh Ricotta
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Basil
  • 70g Finely Grated Parmesan
  • 575g Bottle Primavera Pasta Sauce
  • 85g Coarsely Grated Mozzarella
  • *Cooked Polenta
Procedure
  1. Preheat oven 400Â°F. Spray 2 large baking trays with olive oil lightly.
  2. Cut eggplants into sixteen 1cm-think slices lengthways. Arrange the eggplant in a single layer on the prepared trays.
  3. Bake in oven for 10 minutes or until golden.
  4. Meanwhile, place the ricotta, basil, and 3/4 of the parmesan in a medium bowl. Stir until well combined. Taste and season with pepper.
  5. Place 1 tbsp of the ricotta mixture on 1 eggplant slice. Roll up firmly to enclose filling.
  6. Place the roll, seam-side down, in a 30 x 20cm baking dish.
  7. Repeat for remaining eggplant.
  8. Pour tomato sauce evenly over the rolls. Sprinkle with mozzarella and remaining parmesan.
  9. Bake in oven for 10 minutes or until the mozzarella melts.
  10. Optional Serve with polenta
